Alfheim

Alfheim, reino de los elfos de la luz en la mitología nórdica

Población

The inhabitants of Alfheim live in harmony with light and nature, as though every leaf and every golden ray recognized their presence. Graceful and serene, their spirits are bound to the ancient magic that flows through the eternal forests.

The people of Alfheim live among fragrant markets, eternal gardens, and pathways illuminated by the magic of the forest.

Mapa

The path to Alfheim begins where the mist touches the silver rivers of Ithilfin, guiding travelers through forests that whisper ancient secrets. As you cross Sylvanholm and venture into Fennalis Forest, the light changes: it becomes golden, almost alive, as though the forest itself recognized your presence.

Reaching the heart of Alfheim is to enter a living labyrinth, where the paths shift gently, as if responding to an ancient purpose. The turquoise rivers murmur forgotten names as they encircle slender towers that seem to watch travelers from above.

Ciudad

The city of Alfheim awakens like a living garden, where every street is drawn with the delicacy of an ancient song. Among crystalline fountains and vine-covered arches, the elves walk in tranquility, surrounded by the sweet fragrance of eternal flowers.

The castle of Alfheim rises like a jewel carved from light, with towers that seem to drink from the sky and walls draped in vines that whisper ancient elven blessings.
