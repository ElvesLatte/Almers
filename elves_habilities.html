Elvish Traits

Characteristics

Ability Score Increase

Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

Age

Elves can live up to 750 years. They mature at the same rate as humans, but their understanding of adulthood goes beyond physical growth to encompass worldly experience. An elf claims maturity around 100 years of age.

Alignment

Elves love freedom, variety, and self-expression, so they lean toward the gentler aspects of chaos. They also value and protect others' freedom as much as their own, and typically lean toward good rather than evil. The drow are an exception: their exile to the Underdark has made them cruel and dangerous, usually leaning toward evil.

Size

Elves stand between 5 and 6 feet (1.5 - 1.8 meters) tall and have a slender build. Your size is Medium.

Speed

Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Abilities

Darkvision

You can see 60 feet in darkness as if it were dim light, and in dim light as if it were bright. You cannot discern colors in darkness.

Fey Ancestry

You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic cannot put you to sleep.

Trance

Elves do not sleep: they enter a state of deep meditation, remaining semi-conscious, for 4 hours a day. When you enter a trance, your dreams are actually mental exercises that have become reflexive after years of practice. After resting, you gain the same benefits as 8 hours of rest for a human.

Proficiencies

Keen Senses

You are proficient in Perception.

Languages

You can speak, read, and write Common and Elvish.
