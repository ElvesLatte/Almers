Mapa

Among its winding alleyways, bioluminescent lights pulse, revealing temples, towers, and dwellings carved from the very living stone of the abyss.

Access to Menzoberranzan begins high atop a treacherous mountain range, where a narrow path winds between jagged crags and mist-shrouded chasms. Only the most determined travelers manage to cross its frozen passages, for each curve hides the danger of a fatal slip. At the end of the trail, a dark fissure opens in the mountainside like the mouth of a sleeping giant. There, far from the light of the sun, lies the secret entrance to the Underdark: the threshold leading to the city of the drow.

Ciudad

The city unfolds like a forest of razor-edged towers carved from the very rock that surrounds it, while a luminous river winds its way through its eternal shadows.

In the deepest reaches of the subterranean realm rises the castle, a fortress of dark stone whose towers seem to pierce the eternal gloom of the abyss.
